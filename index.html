Hi there! I'm a passionate Unity developer with over 10 years of experience crafting innovative and engaging games. Video games have always been more than just a profession for me - they're a lifelong passion that fuels both my creativity and technical expertise.

In my free time, I enjoy remaking old games, blending nostalgia with modern tools and techniques. I also dedicate time to working on my own custom game engine, exploring the foundations of game development, and dabbling in reverse engineering to better understand how things work under the hood.

Here are a few highlights of my expertise:

  • C# Excellence - I take pride in writing clean, efficient, and maintainable code.
  • Multiplayer Development - I've built solid multiplayer systems that keep gameplay smooth and synced across networks.
  • Game Optimization - I enjoy the challenge of squeezing out every bit of performance while keeping the game looking and feeling great.
  • ECS - I leverage ECS to create scalable and high-performance systems for large-scale projects
  • CI/CD Pipelines - I streamline development with automated builds and deployments, making iteration faster and smoother.
  • Editor Tools - I love building tools that make life easier for developers and speed up workflows.
  • UI Implementation - I love coding UIs that feel smooth, look great, and make interacting with a game effortless.

Game development is not work for me; rather, it is an art form, a hobby, and a passion. Creating games, tinkering with new technologies, or fine-tuning to performance, I'm constantly pursuing the creation of lasting memories.
